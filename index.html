About the Founder

MEGHNA

Meghna, a passionate and determined teen, founded Querencia on her 17th birthday after leading the Guzaarish Foundation, a non-profit she ran for over five years.

Growing up in the North East of India, she experienced the limitations of resources and opportunities firsthand. During her time at UWC Mahindra and other national cohorts, Meghna's realization deepened and she saw the stark difference between education and opportunities between tier 1 cities and regions like the North East.
