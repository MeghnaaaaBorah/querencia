Guzaarish Foundation

Guzaarish was born from a simple wish for change. Meghna Borah, a pre-teen from Assam, founded it at the age of 12, starting with an online platform where teenagers from across the country came together to raise their voices and walk in solidarity.

As the vision grew, Guzaarish moved offline—into slums, donation drives, clean-up campaigns, and educational outreach. These early experiences laid the foundation for Querencia, inspired by the belief that real, lasting change comes from both awareness and empowerment.
