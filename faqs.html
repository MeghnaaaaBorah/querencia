Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mission, programs, and how you can get involved.

Querencia is an organization dedicated to making essential 21st-century skills accessible to young minds in India, particularly high school and university students in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities. We aim to bridge the gap by providing mentorship and skills needed to thrive in today's fast-paced world.

"Querencia" is a Spanish word referring to a place where one feels safe, strong, and authentic; a place from which one's strength of character is drawn. This embodies our mission to help students find their inner strength and build their future.

Mission: To empower students from Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities with crucial 21st-century skills and mentorship, enabling them to excel in a rapidly evolving global landscape.
Vision: To create a future where every young individual in India, regardless of their geographical location, has equitable access to quality education and opportunities that foster holistic development and career readiness.

Querencia primarily serves high school and university students, especially those residing in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities across India. However, we are present for everyone, irrespective of any background.

We focus on crucial 21st-century skills, including but not limited to communication, leadership, critical thinking, problem-solving, digital literacy, and collaboration. Our curriculum is designed to be practical and industry-relevant.

Yes, a detailed General Curriculum Overview is available for download on our About Us page. This document outlines the core modules and learning objectives of our programs.

Our programs are delivered through a blend of workshops, mentorship programs, interactive events, and practical projects, designed to be engaging and effective.

We offer both online and offline programs, including in-person events and virtual mentorship, to ensure accessibility for students in various locations.

All our upcoming events are listed on the "Events" page of our website. Please check regularly for new announcements and registration details.

We offer various internship roles, including Research Intern, Content Creation Intern, Social Media Marketing Intern, and Campus Ambassador, among others. These roles are designed to provide practical experience and skill development.

While specific qualifications may by role, a general requirement for all internships is that applicants must have attended at least one Querencia event. Other common qualifications include strong communication skills, relevant academic background, and a passion for our mission.

Details on how to apply for each internship role, including application forms and deadlines, are provided on the "Internship Opportunities" page of our website.

You can reach us via email at querencia.tgf@gmail.com. We are also active on social media.

You can connect with us on Instagram and LinkedIn. Links to our profiles are available in the footer of our website.

You can support us by spreading the word, attending our events, applying for internships, or exploring partnership opportunities. Please contact us to discuss how you can contribute.
